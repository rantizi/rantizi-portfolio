## Playful Tone Rule

Use playful copy for the certificate section, but keep it lighter than the project section.

Good playful:

- “tiny proof shelf”
- “learning receipts”
- “messy rows slowly become cleaner decisions”
- “how applications live beyond localhost”
- “invisible checklists behind a smooth event”

Too playful for certificates:

- jokes in every card
- making the certificate sound bigger than it is
- hiding the issuer, year, or role behind cute wording
- overclaiming expertise from a short course

Best direction: certificate facts stay clean and direct; the description adds warmth.
