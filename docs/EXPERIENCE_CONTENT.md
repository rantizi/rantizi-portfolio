## Playful Tone Rule for Experience Section

Experience copy should be a little warmer than a LinkedIn description, but still clearer than a casual caption.

Good direction:

- Keep role, company, year, and tools very clear.
- Use playful lines only after the important information is already stated.
- Make bullets achievement-focused, not joke-focused.
- Let the “Tiny Human Detail” carry the personality.

Avoid:

- Too many jokes in the bullet points.
- Vague claims like “worked on many things.”
- Overclaiming impact without proof.
- Making internship/training entries sound bigger than they are.

Best direction: professional structure first, warm personality second.
